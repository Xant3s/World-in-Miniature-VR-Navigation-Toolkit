**Description**  
Draws the miniature model semi-transparently to mitigate issues with occlusion.

**How to Use in Game**  
 -

**Setup**  
Check 'Semi-Transparent' in the miniature model inspector to enable.

**Configuration**  
Use 'Transparency' slider to choose transparency.
